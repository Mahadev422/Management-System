## **2. Getting Started** {#getting-started}

### **2.1 Accessing the Platform**
1. Navigate to `[your-institution-url]/campus-connect`
2. Click **"Login"** in the top-right corner
3. Use your institutional email credentials
4. First-time users will be prompted to complete their profile

### **2.2 First-Time Setup**
1. **Complete Profile**: Fill in your department, year, and contact details
2. **Set Privacy**: Choose what information is visible to others
3. **Explore Clubs**: Browse and join clubs relevant to your interests
4. **Tour Guide**: Take the interactive tutorial (recommended)

## **5. Clubs & Committees** {#clubs--committees}

### **5.1 Finding Clubs**
1. Navigate to **"Clubs"** in the main menu
2. Use:
   - **Search bar** for specific clubs
   - **Filters** by category, department, or size
   - **Sort** by popularity or activity level
3. Click any club card for detailed view

### **5.2 Joining a Club**
1. Open club details page
2. Click **"Join Club"** button
3. Select membership type (Regular/Volunteer)
4. Some clubs may require approval from club heads

### **5.3 Managing Your Club (Club Heads Only)**
1. Navigate to your club dashboard
2. **Members Tab**: Approve/remove members, assign roles
3. **Settings Tab**: Update club description, logo, visibility
4. **Events Tab**: View all club events
5. **Analytics Tab**: Track club engagement metrics

## **6. Event Management** {#event-management}

### **6.1 Event Lifecycle**
```
Draft â†’ Submitted for Approval â†’ [Approved/Rejected] â†’ Published â†’ Completed
```

### **6.2 Creating an Event**
1. Click **"Create Event"** button (floating action button or in Events page)
2. **Step 1 - Basic Info**:
   - Event title and description
   - Date(s) and time
   - Event type (Workshop, Seminar, Competition, etc.)
   - Expected attendance
3. **Step 2 - Collaboration**:
   - Select organizing clubs
   - Assign coordinators
   - Add co-organizers from other clubs
4. **Step 3 - Resources**:
   - Request rooms/venues
   - Add equipment needs
   - Specify setup requirements
5. **Step 4 - Budget**:
   - Estimated expenses
   - Funding sources
   - Attach quotations if available
6. **Step 5 - Review & Submit**:
   - Preview event page
   - Submit for approval

### **6.3 Event Approval Process**
- **Club Heads**: Submit event to faculty advisor
- **Faculty Advisors**: Review and approve/reject with comments
- **Admin**: Final approval for high-budget events (>$500)
- **Automatic Approval**: Small events with minimal resources

### **6.4 Managing Events**
- **View All Events**: Calendar or list view
- **Filter by**: Status, date, club, or type
- **Quick Actions**:
  - Edit (if in draft/pending)
  - Cancel (with reason)
  - Duplicate (for recurring events)
  - Export attendees list

### **6.5 Attending Events**
1. Browse published events
2. Click event for details
3. Click **"Register"** or **"Attend"** button
4. Receive confirmation email
5. Add to personal calendar (Google/Outlook integration)

## **7. Resource Booking** {#resource-booking}

### **7.1 Available Resources**
- **Rooms**: Classrooms, auditoriums, labs, meeting rooms
- **Equipment**: Projectors, sound systems, cameras, furniture
- **Services**: Technical support, cleaning, security

### **7.2 Booking Process**
1. Navigate to **"Resources"** page
2. **Select Resource Type**: Use category filters
3. **Check Availability**:
   - Calendar view shows booked slots
   - Green = available, Red = booked
4. **Create Booking**:
   - Select date and time slots
   - Enter purpose and expected attendees
   - Add special requirements
5. **Submit**:
   - Auto-approved for simple resources
   - Pending approval for high-demand items

### **7.3 Booking Rules**
- **Maximum Advance Booking**: 30 days
- **Duration Limits**: Varies by resource
- **Cancellation**: Up to 24 hours before
- **Repeated Bookings**: Limited to prevent hoarding
- **Penalties**: Late returns or damages may affect future bookings

### **7.4 Managing Your Bookings**
- **View**: All your bookings in "My Bookings" section
- **Modify**: Change time/details (subject to availability)
- **Cancel**: Free up slots for others
- **History**: View past bookings and usage

## **9. FAQs** {#faqs}

### **Q1: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page, enter your institutional email, follow the link in the email.

### **Q2: Can I be part of multiple clubs?**
A: Yes! There's no limit to club memberships, but you can only head up to 2 clubs simultaneously.

### **Q3: What happens if my event is rejected?**
A: You'll receive a notification with rejection reasons. You can modify and resubmit or contact the approver for clarification.

### **Q4: How far in advance can I book resources?**
A: Most resources can be booked up to 30 days in advance. High-demand items may have shorter windows.

### **Q5: Can I cancel an event after approval?**
A: Yes, but you must provide a reason. Frequent cancellations may affect future approvals.

### **Q6: Who can see my profile information?**
A: Depends on your privacy settings. Default is "Internal" (visible to club members). Adjust in Profile â†’ Privacy Settings.

### **Q7: How do I report a technical issue?**
A: Use the "Report Issue" button in the footer or email `support@campusconnect.edu`

### **Q8: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Currently web-only, but mobile-optimized. Mobile app is planned for next semester.
